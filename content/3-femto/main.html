    Not just a smart bulb.

    • It keeps you safe too.

      The unique integration of multiple sensors inside the bulb makes it possible to keep you and your home safe without the need to replace batteries. Say goodbye to chirps in the middle of the night.

      • Smoke Alarm

        Distinguishes different sources of smoke, resulting in fewer false alarms.

      • CO Alarm

        Detects potential life-threatening carbon monoxide (CO) levels using an ultra-small sensor with a 10 year lifespan.

      • Temperature

        Senses the temperature of its environment and responds to quickly rising temperatures.

      • Humidity

        Measures relative humidity and warns you when levels are above or below recommended values.

    • Industry‑leading LEDs

      Illuminate your rooms and help the environment with cutting edge energy-efficient LEDs that provide you warm white light up to 810 lumens while consuming only 10% of the energy compared to incandescent bulbs.

    • Control it all in one app.

      Keep an eye on the current air quality throughout your home, dim the lights, temporarily disable or hush an alarm, and receive mobile notifications in case of danger. All in one place, from anywhere in the world.

    • Want to know even more?

      Gain further insight into your rooms with interactive charts, allowing you to check past carbon monoxide, temperature, and humidity values in multiple time frames.
